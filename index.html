Understanding Betting Exchange

On a betting exchange, you're not just a bettor - you're a maker. Creating your own odds means you can set the price you want to bet at, whether you're backing (betting for) or laying (betting against) an outcome. This gives you unprecedented control and often better value than traditional bookmakers.

Note: A 10% commission is charged on net winnings.
